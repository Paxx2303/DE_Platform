## Detailed Indicators

### 5. Economy

#### **5.2** — New business survival rate per 100,000 population

- **Indicator ID:** 5.2
- **Theme:** Economy
- **Description:** New business survival rate per 100,000 population
- **Formula:** = (Number of new businesses surviving after 2 years / Total number of new businesses registered in last 2 years) x 100. Then express per 100,000 population.

#### **5.1** — Percentage of service contracts providing city services that contain an open data policy

- **Indicator ID:** 5.1
- **Theme:** Economy
- **Description:** Percentage of service contracts providing city services that contain an open data policy
- **Formula:** = (Number of service contracts containing an open data policy / Total number of service contracts in the city) x 100

#### **5.4** — Percentage of the workforce working in the education, research and development sector

- **Indicator ID:** 5.4
- **Theme:** Economy
- **Description:** Percentage of the workforce working in the education, research and development sector
- **Formula:** = (Number of city residents employed in education, research and development sectors / City's total workforce) x 100

#### **5.3** — Percentage of workforce working in the Information and Communication Technology (ICT) sector

- **Indicator ID:** 5.3
- **Theme:** Economy
- **Description:** Percentage of workforce working in the Information and Communication Technology (ICT) sector
- **Formula:** = (Number of city residents employed in ICT sector / City's total workforce) x 100

### 6. Education

#### **6.3** — Number of science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) higher education degrees per 100,000 population

- **Indicator ID:** 6.3
- **Theme:** Education
- **Description:** Number of science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) higher education degrees per 100,000 population
- **Formula:** = (Number of people holding a STEM higher education degree / 1/100,000 of total city population)

#### **6.2** — Number of computers, laptops, tablets or other digital learning devices available per 1,000 students

- **Indicator ID:** 6.2
- **Theme:** Education
- **Description:** Number of computers, laptops, tablets or other digital learning devices available per 1,000 students
- **Formula:** = (Total number of digital learning devices with internet access / 1/1,000 of total elementary and secondary school population). Report separately for primary and secondary.

#### **6.1** — Percentage of the city's population with professional proficiency in more than one language

- **Indicator ID:** 6.1
- **Theme:** Education
- **Description:** Percentage of the city's population with professional proficiency in more than one language
- **Formula:** = (Number of people with professional proficiency in more than one language / Total city population) x 100

### 7. Energy

#### **7.3** — Electrical and thermal energy (GJ) generated from solid waste or liquid waste processing per capita per year

- **Indicator ID:** 7.3
- **Theme:** Energy
- **Description:** Electrical and thermal energy (GJ) generated from solid waste or liquid waste processing per capita per year
- **Formula:** = (Total GJ of electrical and thermal energy generated from solid waste or liquid waste processing / Total city population) per year

#### **7.1** — Percentage of electrical and thermal energy generated from wastewater treatment, solid waste and other liquid waste treatment as share of city's total energy mix

- **Indicator ID:** 7.1
- **Theme:** Energy
- **Description:** Percentage of electrical and thermal energy generated from wastewater treatment, solid waste and other liquid waste treatment as share of city's total energy mix
- **Formula:** = (Total GJ of electrical and thermal energy from wastewater, solid waste, liquid waste treatment and waste heat / Total city energy demand in GJ) x 100

#### **7.5** — Storage capacity of city energy grid per total city energy consumption

- **Indicator ID:** 7.5
- **Theme:** Energy
- **Description:** Storage capacity of city energy grid per total city energy consumption
- **Formula:** = (Total energy that can be stored annually in electricity and thermal networks in GJ / Total city energy consumption in GJ)

#### **7.2** — Electrical and thermal energy (GJ) generated from wastewater treatment per capita per year

- **Indicator ID:** 7.2
- **Theme:** Energy
- **Description:** Electrical and thermal energy (GJ) generated from wastewater treatment per capita per year
- **Formula:** = (Total GJ of electrical and thermal energy generated from wastewater treatment / Total city population) per year

#### **7.4** — Percentage of city electricity produced through decentralized electricity production systems

- **Indicator ID:** 7.4
- **Theme:** Energy
- **Description:** Percentage of city electricity produced through decentralized electricity production systems
- **Formula:** = (Electricity generated by decentralized systems in GJ / Total electricity consumed in city in GJ) x 100

#### **7.9** — Percentage of buildings in the city that have smart energy meters

- **Indicator ID:** 7.9
- **Theme:** Energy
- **Description:** Percentage of buildings in the city that have smart energy meters
- **Formula:** = (Number of buildings in city with smart energy meters / Total number of buildings in city) x 100

#### **7.6** — Percentage of street lighting managed through a light performance management system

- **Indicator ID:** 7.6
- **Theme:** Energy
- **Description:** Percentage of street lighting managed through a light performance management system
- **Formula:** = (Number of light points controlled by light performance management system / Total number of light points in city) x 100

#### **7.10** — Number of electric vehicle charging stations per number of registered electric vehicles

- **Indicator ID:** 7.10
- **Theme:** Energy
- **Description:** Number of electric vehicle charging stations per number of registered electric vehicles
- **Formula:** = (Total number of electric vehicle charging stations / Total number of registered electric vehicles in city)

#### **7.8** — Percentage of public buildings requiring renovation/repair

- **Indicator ID:** 7.8
- **Theme:** Energy
- **Description:** Percentage of public buildings requiring renovation/repair
- **Formula:** = (Square meters of public buildings requiring renovation/repair / Total square meters of public buildings) x 100

#### **7.7** — Percentage of street lighting that has been updated and newly installed

- **Indicator ID:** 7.7
